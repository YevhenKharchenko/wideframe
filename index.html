ABOUT
THE GAME

Puzzle Genius: Block Saga blends classic block mechanics with multiple game modes and evolving challenges. Whether you prefer calm planning or fast-paced decision making, there’s a mode tailored to your style.

Align blocks. Clear rows. Avoid overflow. There's something really satisfying about watching those rows disappear when you nail the perfect placement.

We've got the classic 10x10 grid you know and love, plus some crazy bomb modes and sliding puzzles that'll keep you on your toes.

GAME MODES

  • Classic 10x10

    • Drag and drop blocks from the stack onto the grid.

    • Complete full rows or columns to clear them and earn points.

    • The game continues until there’s no space left for the next block.

    • Calm, strategic, and endlessly replayable.

  • Timed Mode

    • The classic gameplay — now with pressure.

    • A timer runs constantly, forcing you to think and place blocks quickly.

    • Clear rows fast to stay in the game and beat the clock.

    • Don't panic when that timer starts ticking - easier said than done, trust me.

  • Bombed Mode

    • A high-stakes twist on Classic 10x10.

    • Some blocks contain bombs with countdown timers.

    • Clear them before detonation by forming rows or columns.

    • If the timer reaches zero — boom. Game over.

  • Slide to Solve

    • Move blocks horizontally or vertically within the board.

    • Align them carefully to form complete rows and columns.

    • Cleared lines create satisfying splash effects and free up space.

  • Stay Alert

    • Blocks continuously move upward.

    • If they reach the top, the adventure ends.

    • Plan ahead, maintain control, and manage space wisely.

UNIVERSAL
STRATEGY TIPS

Random tapping won't get you far here - you've gotta actually think about where those blocks go.

  • Plan several moves ahead to avoid board clutter.

  • Leave room for large blocks to maintain flexibility.

  • Aim for combo clears to maximize points.

  • Keep the board balanced instead of focusing only on immediate clears.

  • Stay calm under pressure in timed and bomb modes.
