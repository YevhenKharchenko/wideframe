GAME MODES

  • Classic 10x10

    • Drag and drop blocks from the stack onto the grid.

    • Complete full rows or columns to clear them and earn points.

    • The game continues until there’s no space left for the next block.

    • Calm, strategic, and endlessly replayable.

  • Timed Mode

    • The classic gameplay — now with pressure.

    • A timer runs constantly, forcing you to think and place blocks quickly.

    • Clear rows fast to stay in the game and beat the clock.

    • Don't panic when that timer starts ticking - easier said than done, trust me.

  • Bombed Mode

    • A high-stakes twist on Classic 10x10.

    • Some blocks contain bombs with countdown timers.

    • Clear them before detonation by forming rows or columns.

    • If the timer reaches zero — boom. Game over.

  • Slide to Solve

    • Move blocks horizontally or vertically within the board.

    • Align them carefully to form complete rows and columns.

    • Cleared lines create satisfying splash effects and free up space.

  • Stay Alert

    • Blocks continuously move upward.

    • If they reach the top, the adventure ends.

    • Plan ahead, maintain control, and manage space wisely.
