Features

Everything we built ties back to that core idea of manipulating these ancient stone systems.

  • Shape the Terrain

    Move, rotate, and slide ancient stone segments to reshape the terrain and create new pathways for the current.

  • Control the Flow

    Guide the living current through carved channels and branching paths to power the ancient structures.

  • Solve Dynamic Puzzles

    Each level introduces new mechanics like split currents, pressure limits, and unexpected blockages.

  • Progress Through Ancient Sites

    Explore ancient locations as puzzles become more challenging.
